1/Business/COVID-19 vaccine trials show promising results/John Smith/9/1/2022/A new COVID-19 vaccine has shown promising results in early-stage clinical trials. The vaccine, developed by XYZ Pharmaceuticals, was tested on a group of 1,000 volunteers and was found to be safe and effective at preventing COVID-19 infections. The vaccine uses a new mRNA technology that trains the immune system to recognize and fight the virus. The company plans to submit the vaccine for regulatory approval in the coming months
